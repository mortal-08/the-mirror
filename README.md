# 🪞 The Mirror

**The Mirror** is a comprehensive personal productivity dashboard designed to help you track your time, maintain deep focus, and reflect on your daily progress. Built with a modern tech stack, it combines a highly customizable Pomodoro timer, a daily journaling system, and robust analytics into one seamless experience.

## ✨ Features

* **⏱️ Advanced Live Timer**
  * Switch between **Focus** and **Pomodoro** modes.
  * Fully customizable work, short break, and long break intervals.
  * Bulletproof mobile-friendly architecture with cross-browser audio and notification support.
  * Distraction-free **Fullscreen Mode**.
* **📓 Daily Journaling & Mood Tracking**
  * Log your thoughts and reflections for the day.
  * Track your daily mood using an intuitive emoji selector.
* **📅 Routine Planner & Time Blocking**
  * Plan your day ahead of time with the "Tomorrow's Routine" feature.
  * Assign tasks to specific hours and categorize them.
* **📊 Analytics & Insights**
  * Visualize your productivity with weekly and daily statistics.
  * Track time spent across different custom categories and tags.
  * Monitor your progress against Daily and Weekly hour goals.
* **🔐 Secure Authentication**
  * Fully authenticated experience ensuring your data remains private and synced.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Framework:** [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router)
* **Language:** [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/)
* **Database & ORM:** [Prisma](https://www.prisma.io/)
* **Authentication:** NextAuth.js
* **Icons:** [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)
* **Styling:** Custom CSS & CSS Modules (`globals.css`, `page.module.css`)
